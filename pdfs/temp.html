IMSLP needs your help.

Help keep the world's biggest freely accessible music score library online [read more]

  • Standard Subscription (Renews Annually or Monthly)

Benefits of a standard subscription:

  • Full streaming access to the commercial recording library on IMSLP.
  • No waiting for downloads.
  • Access new uploads instantly.
  • Automatic annual/monthly renewal (Prices in USD, cancel any time)
  • Knowing that you helped keep IMSLP running and serving the music community.
  • Standard Subscription (Single-Payment and Multi-Year)

Benefits of a standard subscription:

  • Full streaming access to the commercial recording library on IMSLP.
  • No waiting for downloads.
  • Access new uploads instantly.
  • One-time payment (no annual renewal)
  • Knowing that you have helped keep IMSLP running and serving the music community.
  • Special Subscription (Composer Sponsorship)

Sponsor a composer:

Benefits of a special subscription:

  • Full streaming access to the commercial recording library on IMSLP.
  • No waiting for downloads.
  • Access new uploads instantly.
  • Your name on a list of IMSLP sponsors. (optional - your choice)
  • Knowing that you have helped keep IMSLP running and serving the music community.
  • Contributor/Institutional Subscription

IMSLP is a volunteer effort, and we would like to extend free subscriptions to everyone who makes significant contributions to the growth and maintenance of IMSLP. If you've assisted with maintenance or administrative matters or have made significant uploads, and we have not yet granted you membership status, please contact us at membership@imslp.org.

IMSLP also offers IP-based institutional subscriptions.

  • Membership Help/Contact Us

If you have any trouble purchasing an IMSLP membership or run into any technical issues related to your account, please send an e-mail to membership@imslp.org describing the problem, and our team will be in contact shortly. Please include in your e-mail:

  1. your IMSLP user name or account name,
  2. the email address used to set up that account, and
  3. the nature of the problem or request.

If your problem involves a financial transaction, we will also need:

  1. which online payment method you used, either Stripe or PayPal, and
  2. the name and email address used for Stripe or PayPal.

Login problems - Most login problems are caused by a mismatch between your email and username. Please try another of your email addresses to see if that works. If it doesn’t, try clearing your browser cache and login again. You can search the web for how to do that for your particular browser.
